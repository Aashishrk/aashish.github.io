About us

Our objectives are to preserve the rich cultural heritage of India, to ensure a homely atmosphere for Indian students living away from home and to unite Indians and people of other countries by celebrating and learning through diversity.

    • Mission - Foster understanding about India and her culture among the UMass community

    • Objective - To promote service and leadership opportunities
